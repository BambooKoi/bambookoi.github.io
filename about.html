About Me

Hello!

My name is Barbara Nguyen but I usually go by the username BambooKoi on the web. I am an illustrator/graphic artist from Canada. I like to draw cute and vibrant illustrations as you can see from my portfolio. Most of my works are usually vector-based which gives me the ability to resize and zoom in almost infinitely without pixelation.

In my spare time, I like to read manga and listen to podcasts. I am also learning Blender 3D, Esperanto and Python on the side for fun.
